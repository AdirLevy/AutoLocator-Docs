Privacy Policy for AutoLocatr

AutoLocatr is a Chrome extension that helps automate locating elements on web pages for testing and development purposes.

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal user data. All operations are performed locally in your browser.

Permissions requested by the extension (like activeTab or clipboardWrite) are used only to function locally and are not shared externally.

If you have any questions or concerns about privacy, please contact: adirlevient@google.com
