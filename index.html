Privacy Policy for AutoLocatr

AutoLocatr is a Chrome extension that assists with locating elements on web pages for testing and development purposes.

Data Collection:
AutoLocatr does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. All operations are performed entirely within your browser on the active page you choose.

Permissions:
- activeTab: Temporarily allows AutoLocatr to access the page you explicitly activate the extension on.
- scripting: Lets the extension inject the content script that extracts element locators.
- clipboardWrite: Allows AutoLocatr to copy generated locators to your clipboard when requested.

Third-Party Sharing:
AutoLocatr does not send any information to external servers or share user data with third parties.
